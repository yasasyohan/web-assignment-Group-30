Blood Donation- Hemas Hospital, Thalawathugoda

More about that

Hemas Hospital Thalawathugoda along with the National Blood Transfusion Service organized a blood donation campaign on 22nd November 2021 at the hospital premises. Over 100 donors including staff, corporates, general public and clergy participated in the event.


Donation of hand sanitizers and face masks for people with low income - SamajaSathkara.lk Foundation

More about that

We donated two facemasks and a bottle of hand sanitizer to each family while distributing dry rations and a few extra facemasks were provided to families with several children. At the same time, they were made aware of how to use them and importance of using them. This programme was conducted by going from house to house without a single gathering.

As the second phase, we donated facemasks to families in the Mahiyanganaya area, which is about 260 kilometers from Colombo. Here we donated high quality facemasks in blue, light green and pink with the help of our Fundraiser, and if there are several people in one house, we donated facemasks donated by MAS, one of Sri Lankaâ€™s leading apparel companies.
